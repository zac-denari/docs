## Frequently asked questions

### How do I create a Dexari account?
You can create an account by clicking the 'Get Started' button on the homepage and following the on-screen instructions.

### What should I do if I forget my wallet password?
If you forget your wallet password, follow the recovery steps provided in the app. For additional help, contact support.

### Where can I find the latest updates and new features?
Visit the [App updates](/resources/app-updates) page for the latest release notes and new features.
