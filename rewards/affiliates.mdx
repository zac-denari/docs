## Dexari affiliate program

Welcome to the Dexari Affiliate Program! Earn industry-leading commissions by referring traders, influencers, and communities to Dexari. Whether you’re a KOL, community leader, or just passionate about crypto, our program is designed to reward your impact and help you grow with us.

### How it works
- **Direct commission:** Start at 50% of trading fees (spot + perps), scaling up to 80% as your performance grows.
- **Indirect referrals:** Earn from your sub-affiliates with a simple, transparent two-tier system.
- **Fee discounts:** Share up to 15% of your commission as a trading fee discount for your referrals, on top of Dexari’s 10% discount for new users (first 3 months).

### Commission tiers
| Tier      | Monthly volume      | Active referrals | Commission rate |

| Legion    | $2.5M               | 10               | 50%             |
| Centurion | $10M                | 20               | 55%             |

| Emperor   | $1B                 | 100              | 80%             |

- If you don’t meet the requirements after the protection period, your tier may drop (as low as 30%).

### Sub-affiliate system
- Earn from your network! Our one-tier sub-affiliate system lets you:
  - Get a fixed 20% of fees after your master’s cut, or
  - (Coming soon) Set a custom 5–15% override for your sub-affiliates.

### Lifetime earnings & activity
- All referral commissions are lifetime.
- Stay active! Inactivity (3 months without new signups or volume) may result in deactivation.

### Anti-fraud & fair play
- 50% of commission is paid upfront; the rest vests over 90 days, contingent on sustained performance and no abuse.
- For top tiers (Imperator/Emperor):
  - At least 25% of volume must come from new users (last 60 days).
  - No more than 70% of volume from your top 10 referees.

### Extra incentives
- **Dexari point matching:** Earn points based on referral activity (1:4 ratio).
- **Commission match:** High-profile KOLs can get matched rates with proof.
